Let's play a Tic Tac Toe game!

How to Play:
Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in one of the empty squares. A square can only be claimed by one player and cannot be changed once marked.
Winning the Game:
 A player wins if they successfully place three of their symbols in: A horizontal row (e.g., top, middle, or bottom row), A vertical column (e.g., left, center, or right column), Or one of the two diagonals (top-left to bottom-right or top-right to bottom-left). Draw (Tie): If all nine squares are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
